Chuthams.

a designer with engineering thoughts

Life is hard, and technology is supposed to make it easier. I, as a designer, am determined to create innovative products that will provide good user experience and simplify people's lives.

While I was in Seattle for an exchange program in grade 10, I got a chance to visit Google Seattle office, and it was an eye-opening experience. Since then I've always been intrigued to learn how to design and program something that will be useful for people's everyday life. During my first year at UW, I've taken a basic programming course as well as some design courses like introduction to design and human factors design. As a result, I'd like to extend the knowledge I've learned in my classes to the real world application when I enter the workplaces.

I believe that learning is a life-long journey, and I will never stop learning new things. As a systems design engineer, I highly value quality and good user experience. If you are looking for someone to excite your customers, ping me at cchutham@uwaterloo.ca.

Design Portfolio

Tasting Note

Data Collecting App

Tasting Note App is a data collecting mobile application that I designed during my work term at LCBO|next lab. The application is an initiative to replace existing paper forms for evaluating beverage products in order to improve efficiency and reduce data entry errors. I was tasked to design the application by creating hand-drawn wireframes to illustrate the user flow, producing high-fidelity mock-ups using Sketch, and presenting the overall user experience using InVision.

LCBO Sales Data Analysis Portal

Today, LCBO provides raw sales data to alcohol beverage producers, but most small to medium-sized producers have very limited resources to analyze the data. To help with the issue, LCBO|next lab has decided to developed the Sales Data Analysis Portal, which is a service that provides sales insights and analysis to LCBO clients. My role as a designer included getting in touch with users, understanding their scenarios, gathering pain points, and designing a potential solution to address the key scenarios. Then, I created mock-ups using Sketch/InVision and worked closely with the engineering team to create a minimum viable product.

Forget me not

Forget me not is the newest project that I started just before my previous co-op term was over. Are you a person who often struggle to keep up with things in your life? If your answer is YES, Forget me not is specifically designed to help you! With the app, the user can create upcoming events and get notified prior to the event. The app also has an embeded Google map so that the user can search for locations that might associate to their events.

Roundabout Canada

Roundabout Canada is an escape room company, located in Downtown Toronto. During my winter 2016 co-op term, I worked there as a website front-end developer. My main responsibilities were to redesign and develop the website as well as perform search engine optimization. The Google search result of the new website went from rank 19th to 8th, resulted in an increase of over thousands dollars in revenue!
