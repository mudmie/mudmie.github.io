Lemonade Stand

Lemonade stand is a supply-demand forecasting console game.

Skill Developed: C++

The game begins by asking users to enter their name, number of days they want to sell lemonade, and amount of profit they want to make by the end of the game.

Before selling lemonade, users are required to buy the ingredients to make lemonade. There will be market trend information for the user to determine if the price of the ingredients will go up or down, and weather forcast to predict the sales of lemonade in that day.

Calculator Chaos

Calculator Chaos is a web-based educational game for practicing arithmatic.

Skills Developed: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Photoshop

In this project, I have applied the knowledge of user experience design to create a game that allows users to complete the objective by using the given calculator to calculate random numbers shown on the screen.

The users will first have a choice to select the level they want to play. Once they click the button 'GO', the timer will start. When the timer stops, the game will show the final result whether they win or lose.
